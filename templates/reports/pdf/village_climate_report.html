Water Management
  • Irrigation Infrastructure: Given potential water availability changes, invest in drip or micro-sprinkler systems to optimize water use efficiency by up to 40%.
  • Rainwater Harvesting: Construct farm ponds or check dams to capture monsoon surplus for use during dry spells.
Crop Diversification
  • Climate-Resilient Varieties: Adopt drought-tolerant and heat-tolerant cultivars for crops showing suitability decline.
  • Crop Transition: If any crop's projected suitability drops to "Unsuitable", phase into Millets, specialized Pulses, or other drought-resistant alternatives.
  • Intercropping: Use mixed cropping systems to spread climate risk across multiple crop types.
Soil Health
  • Organic Carbon: Increase soil organic carbon through mulching, cover cropping, and composting to improve natural water retention.
  • Conservation Tillage: Reduce tillage intensity to preserve soil structure and moisture content.
Financial Risk Mitigation
  • Crop Insurance: Enroll in PMFBY (Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana) to cover climate-induced yield losses.
  • Diversified Income: Explore agro-forestry, dairy, or apiculture as supplementary income streams to hedge against crop failures.
